Mission Statement

Purdue Science Student Council (PSSC) is made up of four committees: Executive, Campus Outreach, Community Outreach, and Networking and Career Outreach. PSSC members serve as liaisons between College of Science students and faculty. We provide a variety of events to connect College of Science students and faculty and support the local community.

Our mission is to foster meaningful connections with College of Science students, faculty, and the local community through interactive events and volunteer initiatives.
