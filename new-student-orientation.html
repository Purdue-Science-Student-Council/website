Before the first week of classes, PSSC hosts New Student Orientation for the incoming class of the College of Science. At NSO 2022, there were various activities for incoming students to participate in:

-Departmental Tables: Staffed with upperclassmen and academic advisors to answer questions and get merchandise

-Lawn games

-Raffle prizes: Purdue merchandise

-Free boba drinks from Tsaocaa

We hosted over 500 incoming College of Science students with the help of over 30 PSSC members, College of Science upperclassmen, and academic advisor volunteers!

Check back in Spring 2023 for more information on NSO 2023 and how to volunteer!
